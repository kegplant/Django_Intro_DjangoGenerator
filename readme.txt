Provided as is :D
To adapt to your project, 
    make sure the directory on line 1 and line 14 is where you want your django projects
    then find and replace all SessionWords with your project's name,
    and find and replace all session_words with your app's name.
To run the script, navigate to its directory, then type in  your terminal:
    sh djangoGenerator.sh
And voila!
